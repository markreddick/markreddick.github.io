Discontinued Products

We're sorry to say that we've had to disconinue sales of all our Mac and Windows products. This includes iGadget, iRepo and Tunejack.

Due to most people now streaming music and easy backups of their music collection with iCloud and such, sales of our Mac and Windows products dropped so low that it was costing us more in service fees than we were generating in sales.

We had a great time helping out fellow iPod, iPhone and iPad music enthusiasts.

Sincerely,
Purple Ghost Software
